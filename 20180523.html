#Favorite Places to Eat in Berkeley
Berkeley's local food scene is always a treat to explore whenever I have a chance to take a break from studying. Luckily for me, there are plen(tea) of little gem's to explore just a couple of blocks from the university campus. Here's a couple of places I've enjoyed during my past 2 years here:
  • 900 Grayson
    - would reccomend trying their Demon Lover (fried chicken & waffles)
  • Sliver Pizzeria
  • Berkeley Social Club
  • Tasty Pot
  • Seoul Gomtang
    - a bit far, but one of the better Korean restaraunts nearby
  • U Cha
    - one of my favorite places in Berkeley to get boba
