Hi There!
I'm a black & white film photographer based in the Bay Area. I mainly shoot landscapes on my medium-format Linholf. You'll often find me in the meadows of Yosemite capturing the majestic granite monoliths along with my wife and two sons who are also avid photographers.
Half Dome & Snowy Meadow
The snow was still fresh when I ventured into the meadow an early winter morning at Yosemite. The snow was sleeping blissfully on top of the trees and covered the lanscape with a silence that was only broken by the crunches underneath my own feet. I silently set up my tripod and captured this shot on my Linholf. The film was later scanned and developed with silver gelatin.
